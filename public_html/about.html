About Us

SESO has been serving survivalists, Law enforcement and the armed forces since 2018. Together with our customers, we design, test, and manufacture customized solutions for numerous applications. Our commercial product portfolio gives a good overview of our work, but that is just the tip of the iceberg.

We have the capacity to produce thousands of pieces of gear for government contracts over multiple years, and the flexibility to manufacture small prototype batches for field-testing when required. Our strengths include:

  • Design and prototyping in cooperation with the customer
  • Offices, design, and production facility in Canada
  • In-depth understanding of the end-users requirements
  • Expertise in materials and manufacturing solutions
  • Lifecycle services, including repair and maintenance
  • Heavy-duty industrial sewing
  • Laser cutting of textiles and other materials
  • Metalworking including welding of aluminum for load-carrying frames, etc.

For over 6 years, we've specialized in designing and manufacturing a wide range of equipment for diverse clients, including military, law enforcement, emergency organizations, and our proprietary PMC division within SESO. Our portfolio encompasses everything from tents to personalized load-carrying solutions, demonstrating our expertise and commitment to quality since 2018.
